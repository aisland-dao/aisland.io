Aisland DAO

Aisland is a decentralized autonomous organisation (DAO), based over a barge of 72 x 18 meters (236x 60 feet), always in the international waters.

Aisland is the outpost of a future floating city, testing the storms and the technical solutions to stay at the sea permanently.

Aisland has currently 5 permanent human residents, 5 lucky cats and 10 dragonflies. Cormorans are the most frequent visitors.

We make the living exporting web3 projects and other high tech stuffs.

More info can be found in our official media channel: https://news.aisland.io
